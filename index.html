Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to start flying?
Not at all! Our programs are designed to take you from absolute zero to a fully certified pilot. The Discovery Flight is the perfect way to start without any commitment.
How long does it take to get a Private Pilot License?
It depends on your availability and weather, but most students flying 2-3 times a week can complete their PPL in about 3 to 6 months.
Are your programs FAA approved?
Yes, all our flight training programs strictly adhere to Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) regulations, ensuring you receive globally recognized licenses.
