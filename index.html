Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience?
Not at all! We train from absolute zero. The Discovery Flight is a great start to see if flying is right for you.
How long does a PPL take?
Typically 3 to 6 months if you fly 2-3 times a week consistently. It depends on your schedule and weather conditions.
What are the age requirements?
You can start training at any age! However, you must be at least 16 to fly solo, and 17 to obtain your Private Pilot License.
Do I need perfect vision?
No! As long as your vision is correctable to 20/40 (with glasses or contacts) for a Class 3 Medical, you are perfectly fine to fly.
What is a FAA Medical Certificate?
It's a standard health check performed by an Aviation Medical Examiner (AME) to ensure you are fit to fly. We guide all our students through this simple process.
Are your programs FAA approved?
Yes, all our flight training programs strictly adhere to Federal Aviation Administration (FAA) regulations, ensuring you receive globally recognized training.
