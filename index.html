Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to start flying?
Not at all! Our programs take you from absolute zero. The Discovery Flight is the perfect way to start.
How long does it take to get a PPL?
Depends on frequency, but most students finish in 3 to 6 months with 2-3 flights per week.
